Non-profit organization founded by Tesla STEM High School students

Our organization is dedicated to promoting sustainability and renewable energy in our community. As part of this mission, we are currently working on a campaign to convert Tesla STEM High School to solar energy. By switching to solar power, the school can save on electricity costs, reduce its carbon footprint, and set an example for the community on the importance of sustainability. We believe that this transition to solar energy will not only benefit the school, but also inspire other institutions and individuals to consider making the switch to renewable energy. We are excited to be a part of this important project and are confident that it will have a positive impact on the school and the broader community.
Our mission is to promote sustainability and renewable energy in our community through education, advocacy, and action. We believe that everyone has the power to make a positive impact on the environment, and we are committed to empowering individuals and institutions to make the switch to renewable energy sources. Through our efforts to convert Tesla STEM High School to solar energy, we hope to inspire others to join us in this important mission and make a difference for the future of our planet.
Our vision is a world in which renewable energy is the norm and everyone has access to clean, reliable, and affordable energy. We envision a future in which our community is powered by sustainable sources and our actions have a positive impact on the environment. We are dedicated to leading the way towards this future through our efforts to convert Tesla STEM High School to solar energy and inspire others to join us in this important mission.
